👋 Welcome to Errorlyzer

Your cool assistant for decoding, simplifying, and organizing messy error logs, console crashes, and code chaos.

💡 What Can Errorlyzer Do?

🔍

Console Analyzer

Paste your messy console errors and let Errorlyzer clean them up, highlight the real issue, and explain it in plain English.

📁

Log File Decoder

Upload your large .log files — we’ll parse them and surface the key errors, patterns, and what you actually need to fix.

📜

Error History Tracker

Track all your past uploads and processed errors. No need to dig through old files — revisit and compare past issues in one place.

⚡ Why Use Errorlyzer?

Save hours of debugging time

Understand obscure error messages

Organize and revisit past issues

Designed for developers, testers, and anyone who hates reading raw error logs

📌 Tips for Best Results

Paste full console output (don’t trim)

Upload raw log files straight from your app/server

Use the history module to compare old vs new issues
